This 300-seat theatre is the proud home of the Folkestone-Hythe-Operatic and Dramatic Society. Recently converted from the Garrison church, the Tower is also available for hire boasting a well-equiped fully function auditorium and large function area. A true community theatre. 

Visit www.towertheatrefolkestone.co.uk for more details.

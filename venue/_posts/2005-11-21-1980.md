<a href="http://www.sindentheatre.biz">The Sinden Theatre</a> is located in the grounds of Homewood School in the picturesque town of Tenterden in Kent.

The theatre is home of all of the school's productions, as well as those of many outside organisations, including TODS (Tenterden Operatic & Dramatic Society) and The Woodchurch Players.

<a href="http://www.sandgate-kent.org.uk/The">Sandgate Society</a>, based at the Old Fire Station, has a lively and varied schedule of monthly talks and many not to be missed social occasions throughout the year too. If you are new to Sandgate joining the Sandgate Society is a great way to meet new friends.

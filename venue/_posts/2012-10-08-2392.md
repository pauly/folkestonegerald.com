This social club is open to service, ex-service personnel from all armed forces and associate members membership. 
Big wide screen television (showing all major sporting events). 
Weekly varied Saturday night entertainment. The entertainment consists of either groups, cabaret, disco, karoake, duos & solo singers.
Three dartboards (weekly darts team competitions).
Two pool tables (weekly pool team competitions).
Cheaper beer & spirits (not night club or pub prices).
